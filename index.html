Biography

Xuan Dang is a graduate student pursuing her Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Professor Xiaoning Qian. She received her B.S. degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology in 2012.
